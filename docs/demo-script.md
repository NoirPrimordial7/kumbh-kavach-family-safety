# Three-minute jury demo

1. From the opening scene, explain the private, opt-in family circle and press **Start jury demo**.
2. Show four clearly labelled simulated devices on the offline event map. No internet or hardware is required.
3. Advance: Arya Dhumal moves away, crosses the early-warning radius, and Band 02 heartbeat becomes unstable.
4. Advance: separation is recorded and the last-known position remains visible. Open Arya Dhumal’s detail panel.
5. Advance: simulated SOS creates the full emergency view. Emphasize that the prototype does not contact emergency services.
6. Acknowledge and start Reunion Mode. Show the private meeting point, direct line, distance, accuracy, and connection state.
7. Advance as both markers approach. At the final step show the reunited state and resolve the alert.
8. Open **Family** to explain real BLE versus simulated devices and Firebase versus future Nearby Connections.
9. Open **Settings → Research & Simulation** to distinguish the consumer product from the preserved legacy infrastructure concept.

Reset returns to step one. Presentation mode removes application chrome for a 1280×720 display. Reduced-motion preferences are respected.
